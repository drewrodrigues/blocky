Privacy Policy for Blocky

Effective Date: 12/26/2023

Thank you for using Blocky! This Privacy Policy outlines the information we collect and how it is used when you use our Chrome extension. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect:

We want to be clear about the data we do and do not collect. Blocky does not collect any user data. We do not store, process, or transmit any data related to your browsing activities, personal details, or any other information.

2. Permissions:

Blocky requires certain permissions to function effectively. These permissions are solely used for the intended purpose of the extension and do not involve the collection of any information. No user data is extracted or stored outside of chrome storage or local storage.

3. Cookies:

Blocky does not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies. We do not track your online activities, and we do not store any information about your browsing history.

4. Information Sharing:

Since we do not collect any user data, we do not share any information with third parties. Your privacy is of utmost importance, and we have designed Blocky to operate without the need for data sharing.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our extension or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates. Your continued use of Blocky constitutes acceptance of any changes to this Privacy Policy.
